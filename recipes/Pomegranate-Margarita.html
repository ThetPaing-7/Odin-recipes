Pomegranate Margarita

Try the Pomegranate Margarita! Sweet tart pomegranate juice pairs perfectly with tequila and lime, balanced with the orange perfume of Cointreau.
You can use either pomegranate juice or fresh-squeezed juice from the fruit, making it perfect for whatever you have on hand.

Ingredients

  • Pomegranate Juice
  • Tequila
  • Cointreau
  • Fresh lime Juice
  • Maple syrup

Steps

  1. Rim the glass with salt.
    Salt enhances the sweet and sour flavour in the drink!.
    For the pomegranate margarita it's fun to use Himalayan pink salt if you have it
  2. Shake in a cocktail shaker.
    Shake the Ingredients together with ice in a cocktail shaker,
  3. Strain into a glass and add ice and garnishes,
    Strain it into a glass and serve with ice! We like garnishing with pomegranate seeds for look.
